Operating Principles

How I think about building.

№ 01

Delight, don't just satisfy

A minimum lovable product earns an emotional reaction — even as a prototype. Satisfied customers churn; delighted ones tell their friends.

№ 02

Tight feedback loops

Solving problems in partnership with customers is the only way to win. Anything else is a waste of time and money.

№ 03

Scrappy beats polished

Real constraints create real urgency. Chase faster, make smarter moves, and never confuse motion with progress.

№ 04

Tech finds the audience — craft closes

No matter how powerful the ML gets, all it can do is find the right people. The product still has to close the deal.
